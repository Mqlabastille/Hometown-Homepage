PARKS

Central Park

The biggest park in manhattan, you can bike, have a picnic, go for a run, play football, tour on a carriage. I recommend taking a helicopter tour for this awesome view.

Corona Park

Fresh air, a museum nearby (ny hall of science), food, parking. Enjoy quality outdoor time with your family at one of the best parks in ny.

RESTAURANTS

Prince Street Pizza

One of the best pepporoni pie ny has to offer, prince st pizzeria is famous for topping their pie with so much pepporoni that it overflows and litters the sidewalk with it.

Sempre Fame BBQ

Finger licking good BBQ with so many options and great prices, serving wings, brisket, ribs, burgers, fries and much more. You won't regret a trip here.

SPORTS

Mets Plaza

The home of Mr.Met and new yorks very own mets, grab some peanuts and catch a fly by at Mets plaza located at citi field, queens

Yankee Stadium

Take part in the ruckus when a homerun is scored at Yankee stadium located in the bronx
